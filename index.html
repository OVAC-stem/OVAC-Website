Who we Are

October Visual Arts Club (OVAC) is a student organization affiliated with STEM High School for Boys- 6th of October. We grow the artistic talent providing student-to-student Visual Arts courses.

What is Visual Art?

A visual art is work that has visual characteristics such as shapes and colors that most people will be able to not only see, but find emotion or meaning with when they look at it. It includes but not limited to: Graphic design, Photo Manipulation, Video Editing , Animation, Drawing, and Painting. In our club, we focus on digital arts. These are the main software we teach :

Our Vision

The club aims to introduce a new generation of digital artists who possess the innovation and passion needed to excel in their art field, allowing them to make significant contributions to their community. The club also aspires to foster a deeper appreciation for digital art and its transformative potential in our modern world.

Our Vision

The club strives to fulfill its mission by offering unpaid membership to any individual who holds an interest in digital arts or is even beginning his journey in exploring this field. Through this membership, members are offered tutorial lectures and perform assignments in graphic design, video production, and even 3D design.
